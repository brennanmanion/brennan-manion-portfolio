Reading

I enjoy reading educational materials. Reading allows me to learn new skills and improve on those that I already have. I enjoy reading books on programming, trading, and marketing. Reading is like receiving mentorship but on a flexible schedule.

My favorite books on the subject of trading include The New Market Wizards, Trade Your Way To Financial Freedom, How To Make Money In Stocks, Hedge Fund Market Wizards, and Technical Analysis of Stock Trends. These books illustrate how the best traders and best performing hedge funds place their trades, how to chart stocks to identify psychology in the stock market, and how to follow strict rules to preserve capital and slowly appreciate capital.

One of the most notable books on marketing that I have read is The Four by Scott Galloway. He discusses how Facebook, Google, Amazon and Apple have conquered the world through their business tactics. This book gives great insight into their business strategies. The book also reveals trends in history that these companies have leveraged. Having the same mindset of these four conglomerates that is characterized in this book can give every entrepreneur a leading foot in their future business endeavors.

I love computer programming. My favorite languages are Python and JavaScript. I have read books about data structures for both of these languages, how to perform marketing analytics with Python, and how to construct dynamic web pages with JavaScript. Knowing how to program is an invaluable skillset, and I continue to improve my knowledge of these languages.

Reading is great for learning and is a great benefit to my lifestyle. Reading improves my memory, benefits my ability to sleep easily, and extends my knowledge. 'Never trust anyone whose TV is bigger than their book shelf' - Emilia Clarke.
